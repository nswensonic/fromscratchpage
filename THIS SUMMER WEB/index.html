Steps of Epoxy Service:

  1. Grind
  2. Blow
  3. Clean all cracks and movement cracks
  4. Set up the following supplies (on ram board):
    • epoxy kits
    • Flakes
    • 18 in rollers
    • 6 in rollers
    • mixing stick
    • Mixing bucket
  5. Put away grinder and vacuum
  6. Tape off entire garage
  7. Mix paint
  8. Paint edges (with small roller)
  9. Check with client that they like the color
  10. Paint entirety (18 in roller)
  11. Flake while paint is wet
  12. Wait til paint is dry
  13. Take tape off
  14. Mix clear coat
  15. Paint clear coat
  16. Clean up
  17. Have client check out the new garage and give them a run through of the after service maintenance and have them pay the other 50%.
